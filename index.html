Why we chose
the venue

Two years ago, we started talking about what kind of wedding we wanted. Cynthia has always loved the sea. Sunsets, island vibes, but not too close to the sand. Kelvin prefers something simple and cozy. And for both of us, we knew we wanted something small, genuine, and actually enjoyable.

At first, we thought about Bali. But after looking into it, it didn't quite feel right. It was almost as expensive as Singapore, and we didn't really have any special connection to it.

"What about Kenting?" We thought one day. It's a place we both love. When we found this venue through a blog post, overlooking the sea, with sunset views and an open space, quiet and private — it felt very "us".

We visited this April and stayed for a night. We spent most of the time just sitting on the balcony, looking out at the sea, watching the sunset and sunrise, doing nothing much. There were also a few very cute sausage dogs around. It felt calm, easy, and really nice. That's when we knew: this is the place we want to celebrate with the people we love.

Simple, peaceful, and therapeutic — we hope you'll feel a little of that magic with us.

What's at Kenting?

We can't wait to share what's at Kenting and why we love it so much ✨ here is a curated list of things that we love.

  • Piccolo Bistro 迷路小章魚 — Creative Italian-Asian fusion with relaxed beachfront outdoor seating. Love their squid risotto!
  • Village South Sea Cuisine 庄仔内灶脚 — Thai food tucked in an alley, almost every dish we've tried are delicious.
  • Vietnamese Restaurant 山海阿娟小吃 — A simple, local eateries with good fried noodle and egg drop soup.
  • Zeng's 曾家小棧 — Local Taiwanese style restaurant, known for their crispy skin chicken.
  • Tianzhong Seafood 田中漁夫 — Super fresh sashimi and seafood straight from the beach.
  • Kenting Night Market 墾丁大街 — The iconic street food strip!
  • Sannan Cafe 山男咖啡 — Oceanfront café right next to the venue, overlooking the village.
  • Addicted to Café 嗜。咖啡 — Specialty coffee spot in the heart of Kenting town.
  • Wan Cafe 灣咖啡 — Laid-back seaside café by the beach, great for catching sunset.
  • New Cut Warehouse 鈕扣倉庫 — Quirky camping-style bar.
  • ToP Bar 簡單．夜景餐酒館 — Rooftop bar.
  • Longpan Park 龍磐公園 — Dramatic clifftop park with beautiful ocean panoramas.
  • Star Beach 星砂灣 — A once-secret cove with crystal-clear, shallow water.
  • Wanlitong 萬里桐 — Top snorkelling spot, look out for Nemo, corals, and (if you are lucky) sea turtles.
  • Eluanbi Lighthouse 鵝鑾鼻燈塔 — Taiwan's southernmost tip, also great for stargazing at night.
